Programming Questions

There are seven programs provided. Each corresponds with one of the tasks listed below.

  1. Print the squares of every number from 1 to 10
  2. Print the first ten prime numbers
  3. Sort a list of names alphabetically
  4. Calculate the tax and tip of a restaurant bill.

In your response, use your intuition to match each code snippet to the task it completes. For each match, explain why you believe there is a correspondence.
