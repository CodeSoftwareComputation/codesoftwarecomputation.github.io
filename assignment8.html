Encryption

Encryption has been a source of significant controversy recently. High profile cases including the San Bernardino iPhone, the Snowden NSA allegations, and the Burr-Feinstein bill have elevated the right of the public to use encryption technology to national headlines. On Wednesday, April 27, we will debate several of these issues in class.

What To Do

Come to class prepared to calmly and rationally defend either side of the issue. Bring whatever reliable sources you wish, and develop a variety of well-defined opinions and beliefs on the subject.
