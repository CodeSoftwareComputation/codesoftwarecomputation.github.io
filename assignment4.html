Code Talking

The scientists at Acme Satellites have made an astonishing discovery: deepspace radio transmissions that appear to be intelligent in origin. Upon further investigation, the scientists have determined that the transmissions are in Morse Code, and have built a device that allows them to reply. However, the higher-ups at Acme Satellites don't know Morse Code, and want to be able to automatically translate between English and Morse. Your task is to provide a system that accomplishes this goal.
